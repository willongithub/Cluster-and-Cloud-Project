What is the difference between Cloud, Grid and Cluster?

Cloud: is simply an aggregate of computing power
Grid: a grid is simply many computers which together might solve a given problem/crunch data.
Cluster: conceptually it is essentially smashing up many machines to make a really big & powerful one.
